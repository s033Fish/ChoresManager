Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2024

At Phi Delt Chores Manager, we respect your right to privacy and data control. This Data Deletion Policy outlines how you can request the deletion of your personal data from our platform.

Requesting Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your personal data associated with your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to smfisher@andrew.cmu.edu with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include the following information in your email:
    • Your full name
    • The email address associated with your account
    • Your username (if applicable)
  3. We will verify your identity to ensure the security of your data before proceeding with the deletion.

Processing Time

Upon receiving a valid request, we will process the data deletion within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

What Happens After Deletion?

Once your data is deleted:

  • Your account and associated information will be permanently removed from our systems.
  • Any automated reminders or notifications will cease immediately.
  • We will no longer retain any personal data unless required by law.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Data Deletion Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: smfisher@andrew.cmu.edu
