Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2024

Phi Delt Chores Manager ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your username and email address from the Facebook login process. Your profile picture and name, if provided through Facebook.
  • App Usage Information: Data about completed chores and reminders associated with your account.
  • Device Information: Technical details such as IP address and browser type, to ensure functionality and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To log you in and personalize your experience.
  • Automation: To send reminders and notifications about your chores.
  • App Improvement: To understand user behavior and enhance app features.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent activities.

3. Data Sharing

We will not share your information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: To assist with hosting and technical support.
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with legal requirements, such as subpoenas or court orders.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as you use the app or as required to comply with legal obligations. If you request data deletion, your information will be removed from our active systems within 30 days.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the data we have collected about you.
  • Correction: You can request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data by contacting us at support@phideltchores.com.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Phi Delt Chores Manager is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
